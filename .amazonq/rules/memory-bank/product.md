# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
SuccessVan is a comprehensive van rental and hire platform serving North West London areas including Brent Cross, Cricklewood, Golders Green, Hampstead, Hendon, Mill Hill, and Wembley. The platform enables customers to browse, reserve, and manage van rentals with real-time availability, pricing calculations, and AI-powered booking assistance.

## Key Features

### Customer-Facing Features
- **Van Browsing & Search**: Browse available vans by type, category, and location with real-time fleet status
- **Intelligent Reservation System**: Book vans with date/time selection, automatic pricing calculation, and add-ons
- **AI-Powered Booking Assistants**: 
  - Conversational AI agent for natural language booking
  - Fast agent for quick reservations
  - Voice-based reservation with confirmation
- **Customer Dashboard**: Manage reservations, profile, driver's license uploads, offers, and discounts
- **Multi-Location Support**: Select pickup/dropoff from multiple office locations
- **Add-Ons Management**: Select additional services and equipment during booking

### Administrative Features
- **Admin Dashboard**: Comprehensive management interface for fleet, reservations, and customers
- **Vehicle Management**: Create, update, and track vehicle inventory with categories and types
- **Office Management**: Manage multiple rental locations and their availability
- **Reservations Management**: View, confirm, and manage customer bookings
- **Testimonials & Content**: Manage customer reviews and site content
- **Special Days Pricing**: Configure dynamic pricing for holidays and peak periods
- **Statistics & Analytics**: Track reservations, revenue, and fleet utilization

### Technical Features
- **Real-Time Fleet Status**: Live availability tracking across all locations
- **Dynamic Pricing Engine**: Automatic calculation based on duration, vehicle type, and special days
- **AWS S3 Integration**: Secure document storage for driver's licenses and vehicle images
- **SMS Notifications**: Twilio integration for booking confirmations
- **RAG-Based AI System**: Retrieval-Augmented Generation for intelligent customer support
- **Authentication System**: JWT-based secure user authentication with role management

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- **Customers**: Individuals and businesses needing van rentals in North West London
- **Administrators**: Staff managing fleet operations, bookings, and customer service

### Use Cases
- Short-term van hire for moving and deliveries
- Last-minute van bookings with instant availability
- Multi-day rentals with flexible pickup/return times
- Business fleet rentals with add-on equipment
- Location-specific van hire across NW London areas
